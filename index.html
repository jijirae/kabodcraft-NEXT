Announcement

Regarding the future of KabodCraft NEXT

As you may already know, I have been running the server for free on my machine for about a month now. This consumes resources such as electricity, which affects the lifespan of my PC parts, as well as my sleep and time.

Today marks the final day of our private modded SMP. I will be hosting downloads for the world on my website so that you can play it on your own. Alternatively, if you wish, you can also host the server yourself. You can keep track of the changes I've made on Trello and customize it to your liking.

The server uses Temurin and has batch scripts for GraalVM and OpenJ9. You can either set up port forwarding or use playit.gg for the service. I will still be playing Minecraft, and I can still help with FTPing, mods, arguments, installation, and other related questions, even if someone else takes over as the host.

I won't accept any money for this because I did it for fun. However, if you're willing to buy me PC parts in the future when mine eventually break from running a modded 10GB+ Fabric Server, then I can continue hosting.

-Raeji

Thank you for playing!
